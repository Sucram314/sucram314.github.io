##
How to use

Commented lines end with a hashtag
Multiple lines of comments are surrounded by ##

normal lines follow word;wordtype;... use following as reference

to flag a word, put a * at the end followed by the flag message

to mark a word with an exception, put a ^ at the end followed by the exception

to add a note to a word, put a % at the end followed by the note


to flag multiple lines with the same message, surround them by ** and put the flag messgage directly after the first **

to add the same note to multiple lines, use a similar format but with %%
